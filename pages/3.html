*Szegedin goulash*

    STEPS (youtube tutorial)

  • STEPS: Clean the onions and cut it to small pieces, put some salt in, let if fry on a lard or beef tallow until soften.
  • Add meat, bay leafs, new pepper, black papper, powdered paprika and let the meat absorb everything and get done.
  • After meat is done,pour desired amount of water in along with cabbage and let it cook until a cabbage softens
  • Mix all purpose flour with milk and heavy cream 16% and put it in,let it cook for a few minutes.
  • Put one whole whipping cream in it and immediately turn off the stove
  • Serve with the steamed dumplings

You will neeed :

    -Ingredients

  • water
  • Ghi butter, lard, beef tallow
  • Onion
  • Bay leaf
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cabbage
  • Pork shoulder meat
  • Powdered paprika
  • Heavy cream 16%
  • all purpose flour
  • milk
  • whipping cream
  • steamed dumplings
